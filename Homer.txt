Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson (born May 12, 1956 or 1969 or 1977 or 1980) is a man from Springfield and the protagonist of the
animated television series The Simpsons.

He is a crude, ignorant, and slobbish individual, although he is fundamentally a good person and shows great caring and
loyalty to his family, friends and on occasion, to those he barely knows or those he considers his enemies.
